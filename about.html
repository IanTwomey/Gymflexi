Meet our personal trainers


Anna Kirby is an ITEC level 3 qualified fitness instructor with a certificate in Kettlebells as well as diploma in Nutrition. Finding out how she can help others has always been her passion. She understands that everyone is different and what suits one person may not suit someone else. Anna is a very positive and dedicated trainer with a great motivational attitude. She specialises in helping you with:

  • weight loss
  • muscle gain
  • maintenance
  • fat loss
  • healthier lifestyle

Whatever your goals may be, with guidance, encouragement and support Anna will be with you every step of the way. To discuss your goals and how to achieve them or for more information feel free to contact Anna.

Andy Robinson's aim is to create an enjoyable experience for you on your journey to reach your fitness goals. He is a qualified personal trainer with the ACSM (American College of Sports and Medicine) and has gained lots of experience working with professional athletes at the highest level. He has worked with some of the world's best professional tennis players to help improve all aspects of their fitness to reach their ultimate level of performance. Whether your goal is to increase your performance levels in any specific sport, or to reach your highest level of functional fitness, Andy would relish the opportunity to help you improve. If you want to reach your goals, you can contact Andy by clicking the button below.
